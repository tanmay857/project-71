Auditorium

The school has a modern aesthetically designed, centrally air-conditioned auditorium. It is equipped with the latest sound and lighting systems and can accommodate upto 350 persons. The auditorium is used for various intra school and inter school competitions and functions. In addition it is also a venue for various workshops, seminars and conferences.

library

The school believes that every individual is a learner throughout his life and there is no better way to gain knowledge and wisdom than reading a book. The school has three well equipped libraries with a large stock of books including Encyclopedias, Fiction, Reference Books etc. All the libraries also subscribe to a number of dailies, magazines and journals. The system of bar coding has been initiated in the libraries to facilitate the accession process and the stock taking. The school has also introduced the system of classroom library from Class I till Class VIII. These well equipped class libraries consist of adequate copies of 10-15 titles at least, that are carefully chosen, to cater to the specified age- group and provide a wide variety as well. Every fortnight each child is issued a book from the class library, in addition to those issued from the Departmental Library. An in house Library for reference purpose has also been set up in Science and Computer Laboratories of Senior Department.

labs

The school has well equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science,Electronics, Mathematics, Psychology and Meal Planning. These laboratories give the students extensive practical experience to supplement the theories learnt in the classroom.


School achievments

Matheskit

A team of 8 students from the Primary Department won the first prize in “Matheskit” at Army Public School, Dhaula Kuan on 19 July,2019 . The skit revolved around two children who don't like Mathematics. They were shown how Mathematics influences all the aspects of our lives. It can be English, Music or History. Students enacted their role beautifully along with props.

Inter school poetry competition

In an inter-school group poetry competition held at Air force Bal Bharati Public School, Lodhi Road on 17 th July 2019, BAL BHARATI PUBLIC SCHOOL, GRH MARG stood Second. There were twenty teams altogether in the said competition. The theme of the competition was Laughter.

news about school

  • Hormony

    The inter-school event, Harmony, was organized by Bal Bharati Public School, Ganga Ram Hospital Marg in December 2020. The mega event was conducted virtually with an aim to celebrate ‘The International Human Solidarity Day’. Thirty schools from all over India participated in various competitions to promote the Spirit of Internationalism and Universal Brotherhood. The event provided the students with a perfect platform to showcase their talent in individual as well as collaborative fields.

  • Pulse-2021

    Bal Bharati Public School, GRH Marg persistently strives to provide a legion of learning opportunities to the students. We believe that in addition to the standard framework of educational curriculum, there are endless opportunities for students to learn new skills and ignite new passions by participating in co-curricular activities. PULSE offers this opportunity to them each year by building on their collaborative and social skills and exposing them to a gamut of new and enriching experiences. PULSE 2021 is special for us as the activities are a coalescence of Indian arts, crafts and culture. Our aim is to celebrate the diversity and richness of our culture and heritage. We hope this event will create broader perspectives and provide real world skills to all the participants. At a time when our spirits have been ravaged by a raging pandemic, we hope this event will provide the much needed shift and happiness to the young learners.
